Trough the series

  • Ayanami Rei, the quiet and mysterious Pilot of Evangelion Unit-00, has always fascinated me. Her calm demeanor, haunting presence, and profound role in the story of Neon Genesis Evangelion make her one of the most unforgettable characters in anime.

  • Despite her fragile appearance, Rei is incredibly strong. She faces danger head-on as an Eva pilot, even when it means putting her life on the line. Her willingness to sacrifice herself for others is both tragic and heroic

  • Over the course of the series, Rei begins to show glimpses of humanity—her interactions with Shinji, her moments of vulnerability, and her eventual defiance of Gendo. Watching her evolve from a 'doll' to someone who questions her existence is deeply moving.

  • Rei’s enigmatic nature draws me in. She’s quiet, reserved, and often hard to read, yet there’s a depth to her character that feels almost otherworldly. Her calmness in the face of chaos is both unsettling and inspiring.
